Making Machines Make Art

Join us at The Atrium School in Watertown for two, week-long investigations into art & machines from June 15-19 & 22-26.

We'll spend our time making machines which draw and paint and project and splatter and spin - and then making art with those machines. Some will be simple (e.g. slingshots and water balloons full of paint) and some will be complex (e.g. designing linkages that let you draw complex shapes with simple motions). We'll be writing code and building mechanisms and robots to make more complex drawing and painting possible.

At the end of each week, you'll end up with a collection of art from which we'll help you frame and finish your best pieces. Throughout the week, we'll also be working together on a larger scale, spray painting machine which will allow you to create large, spray-paint murals based on drawings or photographs you feed to the machine.
